Service Evaluation and Quotation

Quotation made by our repair technicians is only an assessment based on the fault description you have provided to us via phone calls, SMS, and Mail. Our repair service is based on the conversation from the phone calls. There are cases that the price of the repair of the unit becomes higher than the estimated price. This usually occurs when the fault description provided to us was inaccurate, or in the event that we found other faults in your unit. When these instances occur you will be contacted personally to further discuss the details for the price of repair we find.


All services are subject to a "minimum charge”. The minimum amount charged covers our repair specialist's time and any part used to diagnose to the unit. After a thorough inspection of the unit, the minimum charge will become due. The charges are
Mac Diagnosis - Rs. 1200/-
iPhone Diagnosis - Rs. 650/-
Android Diagnosis - Rs. 450/-


When it would be cheaper for you to purchase a new device than to fix your old device, your device is "beyond economical repair” and we strongly suggest against repair. When we provide a new repair quotation and you refuse this quotation. Note, that return is subject to the diagnosis charges as per the device type. We may waive the minimum charges at our discretion, provided you give us permission to use the damaged unit for e-waste recycling.

Turn Around Time

Repair services usually take 1-3 days from the date they arrive at our service centre, this apart from the time taken to deliver the unit which is beyond our control. during peak season, a repair can take much longer to be fixed and processed. for individuals that require the unit back as quickly as possible, we strongly recommended that you collect your device after the job is done.

Repairs warranty

Most of our repair services carry 90 days limited warranty except for the following repairs:
Liquid damage repair - 30 days warranty.
Main board repair/ logic board repairs - 30 days warranty.
In the event repairs on your unit become faulty within the set warranty period, please send the unit back to us with a copy of the receipt or Ref No. and a detailed cover letter stating the current fault or error you are experiencing. We are not liable for the costs you incur in sending the unit to us. we will conduct a thorough investigation and respond within 3 working days. If the problem is not from the initial repair, we will contact you to discuss the matter and let you make, an informed decision with regard to the cost. We will attempt to repair your unit TWICE after the initial repair. If afterwards, the unit is still faulty, we will issue a refund. Minimum charge and postage fee not included. We will need to check the unit and make sure it's faulty prior to issuing a refund.

Unpaid Repairs

All units after they are either fixed or declared beyond the economical repair or require a re-quote will be held for 25 days maximum. We will make reasonable attempts to contact you by phone and email a minimum of 3 times. If no payment was received within 25 days, the unit will be auctioned to recover the costs of repairs made.

Liquid damage Repairs

Liquid or Water damage repairs are carried out on a "best endeavours" only basis. The possibility of the original fault reappearing is high after the unit has been repaired and sometimes the fault can even get worse over a period of time. We offer only a 30-day warranty for water or liquid damage repair service.

We always try to keep our customers delighted with our services and our complaint resolution system helps us to ensure that our customers are always satisfied even when things go wrong. Legal Complaints

If you wish to file a legal complaint against us, you must do so in the state where the repair was performed. The laws of that state will govern your complaint, and we will not be able to participate in any legal proceedings that are filed in a different state.
